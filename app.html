Game Rules

How the Game Works:

1. Start:

  • Kids receive €20; adults get €400.
  • Kids choose a color and receive 6 matching Situation Cards.
  • Place your detective figure on the starting field that matches your color.

2. Game Flow (clockwise):

  • Draw a Situation Card, read it aloud, and make a decision.
  • Move your figure and subtract the cost from your budget.
  • If you land on a Knowledge Field:
    • Answer a question to earn €1 for each correct answer.
  • Track your budget and color decisions on your sheet.

3. End of Game:

  • The game ends after each child has played 6 cards.
  • Final reflection: “What could I have done differently?”

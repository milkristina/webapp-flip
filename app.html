Welcome to the FLiP's Detective Agency!

Game Rules

Getting Started:

  1. Set Up Budgets:
    • Adults: €400 total (or €200 each if two are playing).
    • Kids: €20 each.
    • An adult explains the family budget sheet.
  2. Choose Colors:
    • Each player picks a color and gets 6 matching Situation Cards.
    • White cards are for adults.
  3. Start the Game:
    • Put detective figures on the colored start fields.
    • Adults play first to demonstrate a turn.

Taking a Turn:

  1. Draw your Situation Card and read it aloud.
  2. Discuss options with others (optional).
  3. Make your choice and explain why.
  4. Move your piece the number of steps shown on the card.
  5. Subtract the cost from your budget.
  6. Landed on a Knowledge Field?
    • Draw a Knowledge Card.
    • Another player reads the question.
    • Correct answer = +€1.
  7. Record your budget and color choices on your budget sheet.

Game End:

  • After 6 cards per player, the game ends.
  • If your budget runs out:
    • Keep playing!
    • Only take free paths.
  • Discuss: "What could we have done differently?"
